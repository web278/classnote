About Us

ClassNote is your ultimate study buddy, utilizing state-of-the-art artificial intelligence to transform your note-taking experience. Say goodbye to tedious manual note-taking and hello to effortless, organized study sessions.

  • AI-powered note generation: Our advanced AI algorithms analyze any topic you input and generate detailed notes with headings and pointers, enabling you to grasp complex concepts with ease.
  • Customizable note formats: Tailor your notes to suit your preferences and study style.
  • Cloud synchronization: Access your notes from anywhere, anytime with seamless cloud synchronization.
  • Offline access: Take your notes with you even when you're offline.
  • Multi-platform support: ClassNote is available on all your devices, ensuring you have access to your notes wherever you go.
